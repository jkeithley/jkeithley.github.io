Inverness, Scotland

Take an Outlander Journey Through the Highlands

Foyers Lodge

Enjoy traditional furnishings and antiques in a room or apartment during your stay.

Wardlaw Mausoleum

Pay your respects to Lord Lovat and Jamie Fraser's grandfather; Simon Fraser, at their final resting place.

The Clansman Centre

Learn about the clansman way of life and view original artifacts, weaponry and clothing.

Culloden Battlefield

Visit the final battlesite and mourn more than 1,000 clansmen lost in the Jacobite Rising.

Beauly Priory

See the beautiful site where the Lovat Frasers were laid to rest in the Outlander books.

Clava Cairns

Visit one of the locations thought to be part of the inspiration behind Craigh Na Dun.

Other Places to Visit
Fort George

Learn all about the life of a redcoat and view a large collection of weapons, clan flags and much more.

Castle Leod

"Castle Leoch" from the Outlander series was the result of inspiration from Castle Leod which is the real official seat of Clan Mackenzie and their chief.

Urquhart Castle

Climb Grant Tower, look down into an original prison cell and peer into the waters where Claire spots the "water horse" in the Outlander books.

Interesting Facts

~ Over 1,000 clansmen died at the Battle of Culloden; where the last battle was fought on British soil.¹

~ There are several stone circles around Scotland. The Clava Cairns has chambers where the dead were buried thousands of years ago.¹

~ The name Beauly Priory means 'beautiful place.' this is where Claire meets the Seer Maisri in the Outlander series.¹
