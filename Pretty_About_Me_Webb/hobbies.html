What I Do For Fun

I don't have a lot of hobbies. I watch a lot of Netflix and Amazon. My main hobby is painting. I have painted a desk my father made for my daughter, painted an Evee evolutions drawing I created, and started a level 3 adult paint by numbers. I love to create works of art for my daughter, friends, and family.

I also play a lot of Minecraft with my daughter. We have recently learned how to add modifications to the Java edition on her desktop. I absolutely love watching my daughter learn; so I suppose teaching her new things; especially computer related, is also a hobby of mine.

If you would like to try out modifications in Minecraft Java Edition; I highly suggest using the Curseforge website.
