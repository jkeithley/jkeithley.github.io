At Webb's Wags & Wiggles Pet Shop we rescue animals in distress. We have contact information on our website in order for those who see an animal in need to contact us. Once we have been contacted we will go out and retrieve the animal and nurse it back to health. Once we have confirmed it is healthy enough to go to a new home, the animals are put up for adoption. All adoptable animals have had all necessary shots and have been microchipped. All cats and dogs must be fixed prior to adoption. If you are in need of a place to bring a pet that you simply cannot take care of, or have found a stray animal, please do not hesitate to call or drop in. We appreciate our customers and all donations we receive go towards our animals care and adoption process. WE ARE A NO KILL ANIMAL SHELTER
